Three directions for a device-aware sortarr

All showing the Dashboard on a phone (~390px viewport). Each direction differs in navigation model, information density, and interaction patterns.

A

Bottom Sheet — Mobile-native nav

Tab bar replaces sidebar. Slide-up panels for details. Cards stack full-width. Warm editorial personality.

Fluid Grid — Container-query atomic

Collapsible drawer nav. Everything remixes layout by container width. Overflow menus. Technical dense personality.

Single-column focus always. Top bar with hamburger. Horizontal scrolling stat carousel. Bottom action sheets. Clean app-like.
